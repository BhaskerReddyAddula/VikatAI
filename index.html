About VIKAT.AI

Cybersecurity-first engineering for cloud, data & AI

VIKAT.AI is a cybersecurity engineering company focused on securing modern enterprises running on cloud, data, and AI platforms. We help CISOs, CTOs, and security leaders adopt AI and cloud securely, responsibly, and at scale — combining security architecture, Microsoft Security, and AI-driven defense.

Our security philosophy is simple: assume breach, design for resilience, and make identity the new perimeter. Security must be continuous, deeply integrated into platforms and operations, and supported by AI that reduces noise instead of adding to it.

From Microsoft Security to cloud and data platforms, VIKAT.AI designs and implements secure architectures, Zero Trust models, and controls that align with your risk, compliance, and operational realities.

Our Process

End-to-end security delivery built on Microsoft Security

VIKAT.AI delivers cybersecurity programs using Microsoft’s security ecosystem. Our approach aligns identity, threat protection, monitoring, and compliance to secure cloud, data, and AI platforms at enterprise scale.

01

Identity & Zero Trust
(Microsoft Entra)

Establish identity as the control plane using Microsoft Entra ID. Enforce Zero Trust with strong authentication, least-privilege access, conditional access, and identity governance.

02

Threat Protection
(Microsoft Defender)

Protect endpoints, cloud workloads, identities, email, and applications using Microsoft Defender XDR with unified visibility and automated threat response.

03

Detection & Response
(Microsoft Sentinel)

Centralize logs and security signals in Microsoft Sentinel to enable SIEM and SOC operations, advanced analytics, and AI-driven incident response.

04

Data Protection & Compliance
(Microsoft Purview)

Secure sensitive data and meet regulatory requirements using Microsoft Purview for data discovery, classification, information protection, and compliance management.
